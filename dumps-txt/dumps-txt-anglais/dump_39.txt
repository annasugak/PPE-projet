            Meaning of give the green light to something in English

give the green light to something

   idiom
   Add to word list Add to word list
   to give [51]permission for someone to do something or for something to
   [52]happen:
   The [53]council has given the [54]green [55]light to the new
   [56]shopping [57]centre.
