            Meaning of give the green light to something in English

give the green light to something

   idiom
   Add to word list Add to word list
   to give permission for someone to do something or for something to
   happen:
   The council has given the green light to the new shopping centre.

   Thesaurus: synonyms, antonyms, and examples
   to allow someone to do something
     * allowAm I allowed to go to the park?
     * letShe wanted to go but her parents wouldn't let her.
     * permitSmoking is not permitted in any part of the building.
     * have your permissionYou have my permission to stay out past
       midnight on Friday.
     * authorizeWho authorized this expenditure?
     * give something the go-aheadThe government has given the go-ahead
       for a multi-billion pound road-building project.
